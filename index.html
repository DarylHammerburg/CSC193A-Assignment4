Valve's Source engine

Source is a 3D game engine developed by Valve designed to provide developers with the most torturous experience possible.
Source is a successor to the somehow even more unintuitive to use GoldSrc engine from 2004.
Source has been used in many titles such as Half-Life:Source, Counter-Strike:Source, and Half-Life 2

You have to use at least four separate pieces of software in order to successfully port a 3D model into source,
and may god have mercy on your soul if you want to bake a lightmap onto a translucent texture.
